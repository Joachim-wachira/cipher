HISTORY

Cipher is a Saudi-based cybersecurity startup specializing in risk assessment, penetration testing, threat detection, and incident response. With over 3 years of expertise, the company offers comprehensive security solutions to protect digital assets. In November 2024, Cipher secured a SAR 50 million ($13.3 million) pre-IPO investment from Impact46 to drive technological advancements and global expansion. The startup operates a Managed Security Operations Center (SOC) to enhance digital infrastructure protection, reflecting its commitment to innovation in cybersecurity.
